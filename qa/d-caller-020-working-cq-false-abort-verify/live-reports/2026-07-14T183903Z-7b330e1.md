## Sequence exercised

1. Synthesise 4 FT8 signals fresh (`qa/rr-study/synth_wav.py`): the partner's CQ rendered twice
   with different seeds (`CQ Q1TST JO22`), a response to our own CQ (`Q1OFZ Q1TST JO22`), and a
   genuine third-party message (`Q2OTR Q1TST +03`).
2. Decode all four with the real `Ft8Decoder` — all four recovered correctly.
3. **`QsoAnswererService`:** engage on the partner's CQ (→ `WaitReport`) → feed the partner's
   *second* CQ render (the re-transmission) → **must not abort** → feed the genuine third-party
   message → **must abort to `Idle`** (control, AC-2).
4. **`QsoCallerService`:** prime into `WaitAnswer`/`WaitReport` → partner responds (→ `WaitRr73`)
   → feed the partner's CQ re-transmission → **must not abort** → feed the genuine third-party
   message → **must abort to `Idle`** (control, AC-4).
